How it works

Our analyzer uses a headless browser to visit your specified website and extract all cookies. It then performs a comprehensive security analysis, checking for common vulnerabilities and providing actionable recommendations to improve your website's cookie security posture.

Privacy Notice: We don't store any data from analyzed websites. All analysis is performed in real-time and results are not saved.
